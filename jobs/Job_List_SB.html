Location: Toronto, ON, CA

Scotiabank has begun the journey to modernize both development practices and tools. One of the areas being explored is the public cloud and the various platform technologies that support both development and operations on the cloud. The aim is to reduce costs by providing a streamlined process and framework which will allow the development team to focus on building business logic. We are looking to build our development team with influencers, makers, creators and industry leaders who will drive us forward and enhance the experience of our customers.

The role of Network Engineer / Architect will allow you to have the opportunity to combine your technical ability, strategic thinking and provide detail-oriented execution in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. You will provide critical support, and act as a technical advisor performing research and providing guidance to PAAS as platform and public/private cloud projects, and regularly reviews technical standards making recommendations to ensure they continue to meet business and telecom industry requirements. You will join a new team with the purpose of transforming both development and operations. The team has a strong focus on being people first and promotes support and training on both an individual and a team level.

THINGS YOU WILL DO:

  • Work on a hybrid cloud environment (public cloud and private cloud) and identify, analyze, and design traffic flows and various controls and appliances
  • Analyze and design security solutions for applications and infrastructure, and provide expertise and consulting to internal clients;
  • Develop, recommend, and gain support for Technical Standards to meet the telecommunications industry and business requirements of the business units (especially PAAS as platform and cloud teams), including producing and maintaining the technical standards documentation and ensuring the project implementation process complies with them. They are required to socialize and get agreement within the team and with other groups for new standards of small to medium complexity.
  • Perform support activities required to maintain the department’s operational objectives. In addition to carry a pager to respond to alerts this includes activities such as identify opportunities to cleanup legacy cabling/hardware/configurations to release capacity or enhance monitoring/performance.
  • Perform a technical consultant and co-ordination role for externally driven projects, and actively be involved in the research, design, planning, development and/or evaluation of new network technologies to support customer and business objectives.
  • Manage complex or multiple projects requiring strong analytical skills. Thoroughly analyze technical problems and implement solutions by developing detailed work plans, scheduling resources, gaining approvals, and monitoring progress to meet required standards of the network department and business unit goals.
  • Provide on call support to assist with problem resolution and ensure networks are operational according to documented user service level agreements.
  • Provides advice and guidance to management with regards to future network trends. Identify and pursue opportunities to enhance expertise and knowledge and identify and address areas for personal development in order to provide a high level of management and technical advice and guidance. Provides technical expertise, guidance, advice and knowledge transfer to all network staff.

YOUR BACKGROUND AND SKILLS INCLUDE:

  • Public/Private Cloud deployment experience (especially Azure, AWS, or GCP)
  • Understanding of internal Unix systems and networking (DNS, TCP/IP, UDP, etc)
  • Good understanding of the business and business systems supported
  • Superior problem solving and decision-making skills to resolve work issues
  • Good communication both verbal and written and good interpersonal skills to build relationships with internal and external customers and vendors
  • Team player; able to give and accept constructive criticism to proposed solutions in an interactive setting
  • The ability to work under pressure in a dynamic environment
  • Require a minimum of 7 years’ industry experience
  • Excellent communications and interpersonal skills to deal with a wide variety of user groups
  • Knowledge on Infoblox, F5 load balancer, gateway firewalls and Software Defined Network will be an asset
  • Require wide variety of skills and expertise regarding voice and data communications equipment, configuration, and protocols
  • Project management and follow-up documentation skills

EDUCATION:

  • Bachelor degree in Science, Engineering or computer science or diploma in networking technology.
  • Degree or equivalent courses (CISCO ertified) in communications technology and advanced electronics.
