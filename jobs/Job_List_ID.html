Job Description

WHAT YOU’LL DO:

As the Junior Database Developer, you will be responsible for the design and development of core database schema and logic to support application development teams. This role has a close relationship with various business teams to support business operations and strategy with data extracts, report generations and the analysis of large data sets to provide business criteria. In addition, this role helps define and improve standards for database development practices, deployment practices and documentation. This includes:

  • Propose design, finalize design and implement database schema and logic to meet project requirements
  • Participate in code reviews to ensure proper coding and security practices are followed
  • Support deployment and stabilization of projects
  • Support the elaboration of potential features and business requests
  • Responsible for the generation of data extracts and reports to meet business requirements
  • Participate in code reviews to ensure proper coding and security practices are followed
  • Support deployment and stabilization of projects
  • Support the elaboration of potential features and business requests
  • Maintain current and evolving practices including defining data dictionaries, documentation, development standards and deployment processes (automation/devops)
  • Participate in performance tuning and troubleshoot performance issues to ensure the health of Indigo systems
  • Stay current in technical knowledge and software development practices while consistently gaining an understanding of business processes, logical data models and relational database implementations
  • Act as an advocate for the customer by placing them at the forefront of all decision-making and design processes
  • Proactively identify and anticipate customer expectations and needs
  • Embrace and seek out technology that creates high tech and high touch solutions for Indigo’s customers
  • Challenge the status quo by consistently identifying areas for improvement, diagnosing issues and working to resolve them

Qualifications

WHO YOU ARE:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science or a related program
  • 1+ years of database development using T-SQL on SQL Server
  • 1+ Years of Database design and architecture experience
  • Strong knowledge of schema design, stored procedures, SSIS/DTS development, query profiling and optimization
  • Understanding of indexing strategies, replication and data extraction and manipulation
  • Previous experience with large scale data processing and optimization for performance and scalability
  • Computer Science degree or related formal computer training
  • Demonstrated experience learning and evaluating new technologies
  • Experience with C#/.NET 3.5 (or later) with Visual Studio an asset
  • Microsoft certifications are an asset
  • Familiarity with NoSQL data stores is a bonus
  • Strong analytic and problem-solving capabilities
  • Ability to work autonomously and take ownership for large/complex features/systems
  • Must have strong organizational skills to facilitate the coordination and completion of multiple projects, as well as the communication skills to work with multiple teams
  • Proven ability to work in a team setting and support a collaborative and productive environment
  • Knowledge of software development life cycle (waterfall and agile)
  • Experience working with source control platforms such as GIT, Bit Bucket, and SVN is an asset
  • Ability to learn business logic and processes quickly
  • Provide a perspective that is not only technical in nature but has the business in mind
  • Attitude of looking for solutions!
