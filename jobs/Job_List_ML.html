Network Security Operations Analyst

Date: Nov 20, 2016
Location: Toronto, ON, CA
Company: John Hancock

Description

As a member of the Global Network Security Operations Team, the Security Operations Analyst, is a highly motivated, results-oriented expert who will be responsible for optimally operating network security products globally. Network security products include Firewalls, IPSs / IDSs, web security gateways and proxies, url filtering and advanced threat protection systems e.g. FireEye. Support of these products is delivered inhouse and through outsourced partners / teams.

This role will liaison with all business units requesting firewall changes to ensure smooth, end to end execution of changes, and participate in projects to enable trouble free roll-out of new applications and functionality as required by the business. The Network Security Analyst will also be responsible to support systems off-hours in case of an incident/problem, a change that requires to be done off-hours to avoid business impact or for meetings with other geographic regions. Expert level knowledge and experience on installing, configuring and maintaining Firewalls, Proxies, Gateways, Intrusion Prevention and Detection systems, Advanced Malware Threat Protection systems etc. is required for this role. The person will manage the work load and priorities as per business requirements. The position will report to the Manager, Network Security Operations.

Job Functions / Responsibilities:

  • Working with business requirement holders to ascertain necessary information is available to make required changes on the security infrastructure to safely deliver business needs.
  • Serves as the Subject Matter Expert (SME) for the engineering, architecting, and provisioning of Perimeter Security, Intrusion Prevention and Detection, Web Access Security Gateways, Advanced Threat Protection systems and services.
  • Transforms business requirements into system specifications.
  • Provides analysis and consulting on:
    • Standards and Policies
    • Network Solutions & Security
    • Data Security
  • Maintains operational metrics for management reporting.

Qualifications

  • 5+ years′ hands-on knowledge and experience installing, configuring and supporting Firewalls, especially CheckPoint, Juniper and Cisco ASA.
  • ISO 27000/NIST/SANS Cyber Security Controls background
  • Strong, hands-on on FW implementation, configuration, management and support, especially on CheckPoint, Juniper and MacAfee systems.
  • Working knowledge on network security systems, e.g. web access security, url filtering, anti-malware / advance threat protection, proxies, DLP etc.
  • Good understanding of OSI Model, TCP/IP protocol suite, network IP subnetting, switching and routing
  • Complete familiarity with ITIL processes – Incident, Problem, Change etc., experience on ServiceNow tool beneficial.
  • Ability to work with team members located remotely and to leverage multiple service and / or outsourcing providers
  • Comfortable to work closely with several teams within the Information Services area as well with the business teams
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Demonstrated ability to effectively coordinate multiple priorities in a dynamic environment.
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Excellent organization and interpersonal skills
  • Demonstrated ability to learn quickly, take initiative, manage time and complete tasks with minimal supervision
  • Ability to work off-hours, as required
  • Some business travel may be required
