Web Designer/Developer

Date: Nov 29, 2016
Location: Canada: Nova Scotia: Halifax || Canada: Ontario: Mississauga
Company: Bell
Type: Management
Application Deadline: 2016-12-13

Bell is a truly Canadian company with a 136 year track record of success. We are defined by the passion of our team members and their belief in our company’s vast potential.
To ensure we continue to be recognized as Canada’s leading communications company, we’re committed to finding and developing the next generation of leaders. This means creating best-in-class career and development opportunities for our employees.
If you’re passionate, driven and find yourself seeking interesting work, new challenges and continuous learning opportunities, then we want you to join our team.
Includes corporate and operational finance, internal teams support, the controllers group, internal audit and risk advisory services, taxation, treasury, corporate strategy and mergers and acquisitions.

Job Duties / Accountabilities:

  • Analyze, identify, isolate and solve system problems (databases, web applications, etc.) and coordinate with key stakeholders;
  • Troubleshooting of user access and licensing issues, and maintenance of Business Intelligence (BI) Microstrategy based tool;
  • Design and continue development of BI Microstrategy tool, including improvement of input interface, and accuracy and quality of reporting outputs;
  • Building and creating of reports, cubes and dashboards inside BI tool based on specific user requirements;
  • Manage requests to modify application based on specific user requirements;
  • Maintain or modify application/servers based on specific user/technical requirements, and continue to enhance quality of source data used;
  • Test and validate system enhancements to identify roadblocks prior to implementation;
  • Perform and/or coordinate beta testing, determine and validate test data, and document testing outcomes;
  • Following up with various internal and external support teams;
  • Scripting and automation of batch files;
  • Answer end user requests and provides application support, including but not limited to live support, and video based training.

Essential Skills / Competencies:

  • Broad experience in software and database maintenance, analysis, and development and maintenance of web based applications;
  • Expertise in Object-oriented programming (OOP), Model, UML, Agile methodologies, and at least one source code management system (e.g. Git);
  • Expertise with the following BI tool: Microstrategy, or similar;
  • Expertise with the following programming languages: SQL (MSSQL), HTML and CSS, VBA, C#;
  • In-depth knowledge of Microstrategy, SQL Server Manager, Camtasia, and/or Tableau or Cognos;
  • Knowledge of MS Excel and MS Access.

Education/Work Experience

  • College/University level education in computer science, IT, or equivalent;
  • At least 2 years of relevant work experience.

Preferred Skills / Competencies:

  • Understanding of SAP P50 Enteprise PS, PEATs and BI;
  • Autonomy;
  • Demonstrable history of problem solving ability;
  • Ability to face ambiguity;
  • Able to work with short deadlines;
  • Able to adapt quickly to changes (flexibility);
  • High level of customer service;
  • High sense of organization;
  • Knowledge of telecommunication industry preferred;
  • Knowledge of Hadoop will be seen as an asset.

Bell is committed to fostering an inclusive, equitable, and accessible environment where all employees and customers feel valued, respected, and supported. We are dedicated to building a workforce that reflects the diversity of the communities in which we live and serve, and where every team member has the opportunity to reach their full potential.
