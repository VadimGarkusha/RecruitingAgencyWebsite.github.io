WHY WORK WITH US?

There's a special satisfaction to be found in working for a cause you believe in, especially when that cause involves something as important as the protection of Canada's natural heritage and biodiversity. That’s just one of the many rewards that members of the Nature Conservancy of Canada (NCC) team enjoy.

Since 1973 and with many years of experience in human resources, Barbara Personnel Inc. is the choice of champions for all your temporary and permanent needs. We are a 100% Canadian company servicing the National Capital region, (Ottawa-Gatineau). We advocate the highest standards for our clients, our candidates and ourselves!

Our team excels in human resources and know very well the needs and expectations of today’s employers. Centered on a team effort, our counsellors always look for the perfect candidate for each position. We manage an electronic database of over 6 000 candidates, all of whom have been evaluated and interviewed by our counsellors.

To respond immediately and effectively to your requirements, our counsellors use this database to do a quick and precise search for your exact needs.

Calling upon the services of our agency is:

✔ To have better time management, no research, no payroll;

✔ To save money. No publicity;

✔ Select the employee that suits you. We will do the screening for you;

✔ Make your final choice from the comfort of your office.

In pursuit of excellence?

✔ We are highly regarded for our commitment to quality, client relationships and search results.

✔ We have a quality management program which include a client satisfaction survey conducted after each assignment.

✔ We operate a computerized database of more than 6,000 employees to facilitate our research and ensure a quick match to your needs.

✔ A meeting room located in our offices is made available to clients for interview purposes.
